About Gyan Group

Gyan (knowledge) is considered as all powerful/omnipotent, a thing one has to strive to achieve not only for wellbeing of all mankind but even for emancipation of its soul.

Gyan is dedicated to Green Chemistry with facilities and technical knowhow available from Research & Development work to bulk production of Speciality Chemicals for Bulk Drugs, Agro-chemicals, Dyes & Pigments, Polymers, perfumery chemicals.

Our History

Gyan started its journey to become a little giant in the field of Chemical Technology by inception of Gyan Technologyin 2008 in a Chemical Industrial township of Vapi, Gujarat, India. Expanding its scale of production, in 2013 Gyan came up with Gyan Healthcare state-of-the-art bulk production facility at Junagadh, Gujarat, India and further consolidated its manufacturing facility by adding up another production facility at Sarigam, Gujarat, India namely Reliable life Science in 2017.

"We strive to become acknowledged Global Leaders and preferred Partners in helping our Clients succeed in the rapidly evolving Pharmaceutical markets."

Custom Synthesis:

Custom synthesis is the process where a molecule is made exclusively for a particular client according to specifications at their scale As a reminder, in the chemistry world the synthesis of chemicals is set up on a single or several chemical reactions process which targets the development of a complex chemical from reactants or other substances. The first step of the method applied is the selection of component parts followed by the development of the possible interconnections to obtain a large part of the substances utilized every day. Even if custom organic synthesis is often performed, it can be used for all different types of substances
