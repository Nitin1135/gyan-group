About Our Gyan Group

Gaining knowledge, is the first step to wisdom. Sharing it, is the first step to humanity.

Gyan (knowledge) is considered as all powerful/omnipotent, a thing one has to strive to achieve not only for wellbeing of all mankind but even for emancipation of its soul. Gyan is dedicated to Green Chemistry with facilities and technical knowhow available from Research & Development work to bulk production of Speciality Chemicals for Bulk Drugs, Agro-chemicals, Dyes & Pigments, Polymers & Perfumery Chemicals.

History Of Gyan Group

Gyan started its journey to become a little giant in the field of Chemical Technology by inception of Gyan Technologyin 2008 in a Chemical Industrial township of Vapi, Gujarat, India. Expanding its scale of production, in 2013 Gyan came up with Gyan Healthcare state-of-the-art bulk production facility at Junagadh, Gujarat, India and further consolidated its manufacturing facility by adding up another production facility at Sarigam, Gujarat, India namely Reliable life Science in 2017.
